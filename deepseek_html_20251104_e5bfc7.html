About BudgetBuddy

BudgetBuddy is a free online expense tracker designed to help individuals and families take control of their finances. Our mission is to make financial management accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial knowledge.

Features

  • Easy expense tracking with categorization
  • Monthly budget setting and tracking
  • Visual spending analytics by category
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • 100% free with no hidden costs

Our Story

BudgetBuddy was created in 2023 by a team of developers who struggled with managing personal finances. We realized that many people face the same challenges, so we built a simple yet powerful tool to help everyone achieve financial wellness.
